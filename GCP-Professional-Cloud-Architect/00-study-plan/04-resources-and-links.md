## Environment checklist before exam day

- Government ID matching registration name.
- Webcam + mic (online proctor) or test center location confirmed.
- Clean desk, closed doors, no phones in reach.
- Stable internet ≥ 10 Mbps.
- Browser compatible with proctoring software (do a system check in advance).
